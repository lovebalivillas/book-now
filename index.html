Job Summary:

The Villa Manager is responsible for overseeing the daily operations and management of a private villa or vacation rental property. This role involves ensuring the property is well-maintained, guest expectations are met, and the overall guest experience is exceptional. The Villa Manager plays a pivotal role in maintaining the property's reputation and ensuring the comfort and satisfaction of guests.

  • Property Maintenance:Supervise and coordinate all aspects of property maintenance, including landscaping, pool care, repairs, and general upkeep to ensure the property is in excellent condition.
  • Guest Services: Provide a high level of guest service and hospitality, including check-in/check-out procedures, addressing guest inquiries, and ensuring guest comfort during their stay.
  • Staff Management: Collaborate with contractors and service providers for repairs, renovations, and maintenance projects, ensuring quality work and cost-effective solutions.
  • Inventory Management: Maintain an inventory of supplies, equipment, and household items needed for the property's operations. Monitor and reorder supplies as necessary.
  • Financial Management: Prepare and manage budgets, oversee expenses, and report financial performance to the property owner or general manager. Ensure cost-efficiency in operations.
  • Security and Safety: Implement security measures to protect the property and its guests. Ensure that safety standards are met and maintained at all times.
  • Guest Relations: Act as a point of contact for guests, addressing their needs and concerns promptly and professionally. Provide recommendations for local attractions, dining, and activities.
  • Marketing and Promotion: Assist in marketing efforts to attract guests and maintain a strong online presence, including managing listings on vacation rental websites.
  • Regulatory Compliance: Stay informed about local regulations, permits, and tax requirements related to vacation rentals and ensure compliance.
  • Customer Service: Interact courteously and professionally with building occupants and visitors, addressing any cleaning-related concerns promptly.
  • Reporting:Provide regular reports to property owners or the general manager on property performance, guest feedback, and financial matters.
