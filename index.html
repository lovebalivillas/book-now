Job Summary:

The Cleaning Service Provider is responsible for maintaining a clean and hygienic environment within the assigned premises. This role involves various cleaning tasks, including but not limited to, sweeping, mopping, vacuuming, dusting, and sanitizing. The Cleaning Service Provider must ensure that all cleaning activities are performed efficiently and to the highest standards to create a safe and pleasant environment for occupants.

Cleaning Tasks:

Perform a variety of cleaning tasks, including sweeping, mopping, vacuuming, dusting, wiping surfaces, and sanitizing. Ensure that all areas are clean and well-maintained according to established standards.

Waste Management:

Collect and dispose of trash and recyclables in designated containers, ensuring proper segregation and disposal methods are followed.

Restroom Maintenance:

Clean and sanitize restrooms, including toilets, sinks, mirrors, and replenishing restroom supplies such as soap and toilet paper.

Floor Care:

Maintain and care for various floor surfaces, including carpet, tile, and hardwood, by vacuuming, sweeping, mopping, and performing periodic floor maintenance tasks.

Window Cleaning:

Clean interior and exterior windows on a regular basis, ensuring they are free from streaks and smudges.

Supply Management:

Monitor cleaning supplies and equipment inventory, report any shortages, and request replenishment as needed.

Safety Compliance:

Adhere to safety protocols and guidelines while performing cleaning duties to minimize accidents and ensure personal and environmental safety.

Reporting:

Report any maintenance or repair needs to the Facilities Manager promptly.

Special Projects:

Assist with special cleaning projects as assigned, such as deep cleaning, carpet shampooing, and high-pressure washing.

Customer Service:

Interact courteously and professionally with building occupants and visitors, addressing any cleaning-related concerns promptly.
